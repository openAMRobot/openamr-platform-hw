# Notice

OpenAMRobot and OpenAMR are project names maintained by the OpenAMRobot organization.

This repository contains the **hardware documentation** for the OpenAMRobot mobile base:
electrical wiring and pinouts, power distribution, sensor and motor-control notes, the bill
of materials, and safety documentation.
